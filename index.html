Rogoznica - The Heart of Dalmatia

Rogoznica has 54 km long coastline and geographic position located in the center of Dalmatia, moreover, it is the most protruded part of the Croatian mainland which penetrates deeply into the Adriatic Sea. With its location Rogoznica represents a perfect location, a great starting point for all excursions - you can make daily trips - to Dubrovnik at south or National Park Plitvice at north.

Rogoznica is a great starting point on the mainland, and even better at the sea, beside of its outstanding location, it is the safest harbor on the Adriatic Sea.
